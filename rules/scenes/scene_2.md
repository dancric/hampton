### Scene 2: City Hall (September)
#### Story
At the invitation of the Newport News City Council, Admiral Reid stays in town to hear and respond to questions about the future of the shipyard. The City Council, led by Mayor Wallace, must determine how to apportion the city’s emergency budget given the circumstances following Hurricane Helen. The city has $100 million in its reserves, and the city council has already voted to authorize spending the entire budget on the city’s priorities.

Meanwhile, election season is heating up, and there is growing demand from the Local 9999 union membership and the *Virginia Stripes* Substack readership to understand who to campaign for or against. Finally, Representative Chase will offer an exclusive interview from the City Council hearings to a publication.
